About Us

San Juan Senior High School is a proud institution committed to nurturing excellence, integrity, and lifelong learning among today’s youth. Located in the heart of San Juan, our school provides a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that empowers students to reach their full potential—both academically and personally.

Guided by a mission to produce globally competitive, service-oriented, and values-driven individuals, San Juan Senior High School offers quality education through diverse strands under the Senior High School program, including Academic, Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL), and Arts & Design. Our dedicated and passionate faculty members go beyond textbooks, inspiring students to think critically, act responsibly, and lead with purpose.

With strong community ties and modern teaching strategies, San Juan Senior High School continues to be a pillar of excellence, where every learner is given the opportunity to thrive and prepare for a brighter future—whether in college, career, or entrepreneurship.

Together, we build dreams, shape futures, and transform lives—the San Juanian way.

Strands Offered

STEM

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) is an educational approach that integrates these four fields to develop problem-solving, critical thinking, and innovation skills. It prepares students for modern careers by encouraging creativity, exploration, and real-world application of knowledge.

ABM

Accountancy and Business Management (ABM) is a strand that focuses on the fundamentals of business, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It equips students with skills in financial analysis, business planning, and management, preparing them for careers in business and commerce.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) focuses on the study of human behavior, culture, society, and politics. It develops critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills, preparing students for careers in education, law, journalism, psychology, and public service.

TVL-HE

TVL-Home Economics (HE) equips students with practical skills in areas like cooking, baking, housekeeping, caregiving, and beauty care. It prepares learners for employment, entrepreneurship, or further training in the hospitality and service industries.

TVL-ICT

TVL-Information and Communications Technology (ICT) focuses on developing skills in computer systems, programming, animation, and web development. It prepares students for tech-related jobs, freelancing, or further studies in the ICT field.
